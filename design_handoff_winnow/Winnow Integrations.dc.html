Connect Calendly
Winnow will open Calendly so you can sign in. It connects directly — nothing routes through a Winnow server.
Winnow will be able to
✓Read your event types & booking links
✓See which links you've already shared
✕Never sees your email content or contacts
When you insert a link, Winnow fetches your event types from Calendly. Only the chosen link is added to your email.
